How it works

Organize your documents, then send a clear, written dispute.

01 · Intake

Share your bill, insurance info, and what looks wrong

You tell us who billed you, what was done, what insurance should have paid, and why you think the bill is incorrect.

02 · Organize

We organize the key facts

We build a simple summary of dates, codes, amounts, payments, and prior calls or appeals you’ve made.

03 · Draft

You receive a dispute letter draft
