Step 1 · Share your estimate

Tell us about your project and contractor estimate.

These questions help us understand the scope, budget, and context so we can flag unclear items and prepare useful follow-up questions for your contractor.

We typically review new estimates within 1–2 business days.

Helpful to include

  • 📄 The full written estimate or proposal, not just a single-page summary.
  • 📎 Any notes or drawings that came with the estimate (if available).
  • ✉️ Emails or messages where the contractor explained what is or is not included.

After you submit

We’ll confirm receipt, review your estimate and concerns, then follow up by email with payment instructions and a target delivery date for your written summary and question list.
