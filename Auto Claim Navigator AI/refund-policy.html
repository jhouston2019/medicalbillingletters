Overview

At AutoClaimNavigatorAI, we stand behind the quality of our AI-generated auto insurance claim documents. This policy outlines when and how refunds are processed.

Eligibility for Refunds

You ARE eligible for a refund if:

  • The AI system fails to generate any document after payment
  • Technical errors prevent access to your purchased credits
  • The generated content is completely unrelated to your submitted claim information
  • You experience duplicate charges or billing errors
  • You request a refund within 30 days and have used less than 3 generations

You are NOT eligible for a refund if:

  • You've used more than 3 AI generations from your package
  • More than 30 days have passed since purchase
  • Your insurance company denies your claim (we cannot guarantee approval)
  • You provided incorrect or incomplete information for generation
  • You disagree with strategic recommendations (subjective quality)
  • Downloaded documents have been successfully delivered

Refund Process

Step Action Timeline
1 Submit refund request via email with order number Within 30 days
2 Our team reviews your request and usage 1-2 business days

Partial Refunds

In certain situations, we may offer partial refunds:

  • Unused credits: If you've purchased additional credits beyond the initial package and haven't used them
  • Service interruptions: Prorated refunds for extended system downtime
  • Package downgrades: Difference refunded if switching to a lower tier within 7 days

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email: refunds@autoclaimnavigatorai.com

Include the following information:

  • Your order number or email used for purchase
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for refund request
  • Number of generations used (if any)
  • Any relevant screenshots or error messages

⚠️ Important Notice

No Guarantee of Claim Approval: While our AI generates professional, comprehensive appeal documents, we cannot guarantee your insurance company will approve your claim. Success depends on many factors including policy terms, state laws, and claim specifics.
