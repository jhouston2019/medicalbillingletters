Frequently Asked Questions

Do you contact the insurer for me? ▼
No, we provide you with all the documentation and scripts you need to handle the claim yourself. Our system prepares professional letters, exhibits, and follow-up templates that you can send directly to your insurer. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal representation.
Can I use this with any insurance company? ▼
Yes! Our templates are insurer-agnostic and work with all major insurance companies. The documentation focuses on evidence, valuation math, and standard policy language that applies across the industry.
Does my state allow diminished value claims? ▼
Most states recognize diminished value claims in third-party scenarios (when another driver is at fault). First-party DV claims depend on your specific policy and state laws. Our system provides state-aware language and helps you understand your options.
What if I already accepted a payment? ▼
If you signed a release, your options may be limited. However, if you haven't signed a final release, supplements and valuation corrections may still be available. Many insurers allow supplemental claims for items discovered after initial payment.
How quickly can I get my documents? ▼
Your complete documentation kit is available for immediate download after purchase. The AI analysis typically takes just a few minutes to process your uploaded documents and generate customized recommendations.
