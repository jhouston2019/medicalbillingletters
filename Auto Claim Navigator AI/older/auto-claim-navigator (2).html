Frequently Asked Questions

What types of auto claims do you support? ▼

We provide documentation for all auto claim types: collision repairs, total loss settlements, diminished value claims, rental reimbursement, and injury claims including PIP/MedPay. Our AI tools analyze estimates, calculate values, and generate professional dispute letters for any auto insurance claim.

How do I know if my estimate is missing items? ▼

Our AI reviews your estimate line-by-line and compares it to manufacturer repair procedures. Common missed items include: ADAS calibrations, blend time for adjacent panels, OEM part requirements, and hidden structural damage. We also recommend getting an independent estimate from RepairPal for comparison.

Can I claim diminished value in my state? ▼

Most states allow diminished value claims for third-party claims (when someone else is at fault). First-party DV claims (your own insurance) depend on your state and policy. Our toolkit includes state-specific DV guidance and calculation tools. For official values, we recommend obtaining a professional appraisal.

What if the insurance company still denies my supplement? ▼

Our toolkit includes escalation templates, appraisal demand letters, and department of insurance complaint forms. You can also use apps like Mighty to connect with attorneys if needed. Remember, proper documentation makes denials much less likely.

Do you work with insurance companies or attorneys? ▼

No. AutoClaimNavigatorAI is an independent documentation service for consumers. We don't work with insurers and we're not a law firm. We provide tools and templates to help you advocate for yourself. For legal advice, consult an attorney through services like LegalZoom or your state bar.

How quickly can I get my documentation? ▼

Instant download after purchase. While our AI processes your specific documents, you can start using tools like Timestamp Camera for photos and MileIQ for mileage tracking immediately.

What's included in the medical expense tracker? ▼

Complete system for tracking PIP/MedPay benefits, medical bills, prescriptions, and treatment. Includes templates for provider letters, lien negotiations, and settlement demands. We also recommend CareZone app for daily symptom tracking.
