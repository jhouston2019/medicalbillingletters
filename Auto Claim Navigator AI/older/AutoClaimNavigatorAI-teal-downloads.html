Frequently Asked Questions

Do you contact the insurer for me?▼

No. We prepare documents and provide scripts so you can send, follow up, and escalate. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice.

Can I use this with the other driver’s insurer?▼

Yes. The templates are insurer‑agnostic and focus on evidence, valuation math, and policy language.

Does my state allow diminished value?▼

Many states recognize DV claims in third‑party scenarios; first‑party depends on policy. We provide state‑aware language; check your state rules.

What if I already accepted payment?▼

If you signed a release, options may be limited. If not, supplements and valuation corrections may still be available.
